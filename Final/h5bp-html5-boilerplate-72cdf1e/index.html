About Sunday Footy

Sunday Footy is an elite, recreational, 6-aside football league started by a couple of retired local players. The league is open to both the most experienced players, and newcomers alike. Sessions are held every Sunday from 4p.m - sundown at the St.Ignatius Catholic field.



Though this is a recreational league, it does not take away from its competitiveness, so be ready to give it your all. In the end it is all fun, so feel free to come out join the experience that is Sunday Footy.
