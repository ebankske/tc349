What I hope to Accomplish for my Final Project

For my final project, I intend on creating a personal website for a recreational football (soccer) league I am apart of. The league is called “Sunday Footy” and welcomes anyone who enjoys the football to come out and play. The league contains primarily men, but some women to participate in some of the games. The ages range from 14-25 therefore this will be the audience viewing my sight.

As I am targeting a teen-young adult audience, predominantly males, I am looking to create a simple, easy to navigate website, whilst still being eye-catching and informative. I want people who visit the website to captured by the styles, fonts, etc. in order to better persuade them to come out and join the league. I think this combination will work perfect with what I am looking to accomplish as the end result.
